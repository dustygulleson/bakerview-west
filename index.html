21-Acre Mixed-Use Business Park in Bellingham, WA

Bakerview West Business Park is a master-planned, campus-style commercial development offering modern office, light industrial/flex, and retail spaces for purchase. Positioned along the high-visibility Bakerview/I-5 corridor, the park is designed for owner-users and investors seeking quality new construction in one of the Pacific Northwest's most dynamic markets.
